CHICKEN

This chicken recipe is perfect for a hearty and satisfying meal. Whether you're cooking for family or friends, this dish is sure to impress with its rich flavors and tender texture.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped bell pepper to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes until slightly tender.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes (with their juice), chicken broth, paprika, oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Return the browned chicken pieces to the skillet. Cover and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can let it simmer uncovered for a few more minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro before serving. This dish pairs well with rice, couscous, or crusty bread.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious chicken dish!
