BEEF

This beef recipe is perfect for a hearty and satisfying meal. Whether you're cooking for family or friends, this dish is sure to impress with its rich flavors and tender texture.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb beef (sirloin or ribeye), sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 green onion, sliced (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, cornstarch, and beef broth. Mix well and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced beef to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes until browned on all sides. Avoid overcrowding the pan; cook in batches if necessary.
  4. Pour the sauce mixture over the beef in the skillet. Stir well to coat the beef evenly with the sauce.
  5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and the beef to absorb the flavors. If you like it spicy, add red pepper flakes at this stage.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with sliced green onions.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy your delicious beef dish!
